Internet Questions

Questions:

  1. What is the internet of things?
  2. What does a browser do with HTML/CSS code?
  3. What are cookies in regards to an internet browser?
  4. What is the internet? How does it work?

Answers:

  1. The Internet of Things is the concept of connecting any physical device to the Internet or to other devices
  2. When you open up a webpage, your browser acceses the html and css code and interprets it into what you see on the screen
  3. Cookies are small text files with pieces of data like a username or password that are used to identify your computer.
  4. The internet is a worldwide computer network that transmits data to interconnected devices. It works by sending messages to other computer's IP adresses. The message gets broken down into smaller data called packets, which are then recieved and reassembled at the recipient's IP adress.
